## Summary

- [macro/rates] U.S. CPI rose 2.7% y/y in November; shelter inflation remains a key driver, complicating the disinflation narrative. (https://www.bls.gov/news.release/archives/cpi_12182025.htm)

- [tech/geopolitics/markets] TikTok signed agreements toward a U.S. joint venture/ownership restructuring, reducing near-term ban risk but raising governance and China-approval uncertainty. (https://www.cnbc.com/2025/12/18/tik-tok-us-sale-china.html)
- [markets/macro] Trump signed an executive order to reclassify cannabis, reshaping regulatory and tax assumptions; sector volatility reflects uncertainty over implementation details. (https://www.cnbc.com/2025/12/18/trump-pot-reclassification-cannabis-stocks-medicare-cbd.html)
- [tech/markets] Micron reported surging AI-memory demand and capacity constraints (“more than sold out”), reinforcing the thesis of persistent HBM/DRAM tightness supporting prices. (https://www.cnbc.com/2025/12/18/micron-mu-stock-earnings-ai-memory-demand.html)
- [markets/geopolitics] Nike warned its turnaround remains midstream as China sales plunge and tariffs pressure margins, highlighting consumer-demand and trade-policy sensitivity for global brands. (https://www.cnbc.com/2025/12/18/nike-nke-q2-2026-earnings.html)
- [geopolitics] Ukraine urged E.U. leaders to deploy frozen Russian funds for defense; Hungary’s Orbán called such plans a dead end, underscoring intra-EU fracture risk. (https://www.nytimes.com/2025/12/18/world/europe/ukraine-zelensky-russia-putin.html)
- [markets/energy/tech] Trump Media agreed to merge with fusion firm TAE in a ~$6B deal, blending political-media capital with speculative energy tech financing risk. (https://www.cnbc.com/2025/12/18/trump-media-djt-tae-fusion-merger.html)
- [science] Scientists identified a hidden T-cell “switch” that could broaden cancer immunotherapy effectiveness, potentially expanding treatable patient populations and therapeutic targets. (https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2025/12/251218074429.htm)
- [science/tech] Researchers demonstrated an “impossible” Earth-to-space quantum link is feasible, advancing prospects for secure satellite-based quantum communications networks. (https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2025/12/251217082515.htm)
- [science/other] Beginning January 2026, all ACM publications become open access, shifting computer-science publishing economics toward author/institution funding and broader global knowledge diffusion. (https://dl.acm.org/openaccess)
